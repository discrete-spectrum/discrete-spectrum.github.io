Robert Ethington is a drummer, primitive keyboardist, and composer. Among his credits, he’s been a member of the ‘80’s New Wave band Unit 5, Tin Huey, and a wide range of projects with Jacob Trombetta. He has also played regularly with Chris Butler, Harvey Gold, Tracey Thomas, and the late Ralph Carney.

His current endeavors include the bands [Glenn Lazear](../artists/glenn-lazear.md), Golems of the Red Planet, and jazz group, the Robert Ethington Quintet.

As a solo artist, he’s made the albums [TIC TAC TOC](https://discretespectrum.bandcamp.com/album/tic-tac-toc) and [BLACK COLLAR](https://discretespectrum.bandcamp.com/album/black-collar).

His most recent project is a new album of improvisational compositions, LOST LANGUAGE(S), with the Robert Ethington Quintet.

Bob also co-hosts the podcast From Akron & Beyond with Nick Nicholis, produced by The Summit FM.
