Memories of Happier and Less Complicated Times, explores the world of fish and chips. It was released on the fiftieth anniversary of the final Arthur Treacher's Fish & Chips restaurant and first anniversary of Arthur Treacher's day in Cuyahoga Falls, Ohio. The title Memories of Happier and Less Complicated Times is taken from a quote by the owner of the restaurant.

Due to the cultural impact of this release, Arthur Treacher's has been able to open a second location in Ohio.
